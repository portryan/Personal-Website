Hi! My name is Ryan and I am a recent Computer Science and Applied Math graduate from UAlbany.

Education
I was first exposed to programming back in middle school on the TI-84 calculator. I would use it to program formulas to solve math equations for me, but would also make some simple games for fun. In high school I took many programming related electives such as Java, AP Computer Science, and Digital Electronics. I really enjoyed those classes and decided to go to college to learn more about computer science topics. After high school I enrolled at Hudson Valley Community College for Computer Information Systems, then transfered to UAlbany for Computer Science and Applied Mathematics.
